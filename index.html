Crucial for Saving Lifes - Blood

Blood is crucial for saving lifes. It is needed for emergencies and for people who have cancer, blood disorders, sickle cell anemia and other illnesses. Blood is also needed for regular transfusions for people who have lost blood during surgery or from serious injuries. Some people need regular blood transfusions to live.
